Miscellaneous Verse

The following are completed, but were more spontaneous and varied than the “Life Studies”.

The Life and Death of Muscovites

Black, undead hands of a loved heart surgeon
Clutch the ever-burning jewel flame
Plastic cover shelters off-white bust
Rain runs down in blots and blurs his name.

Colors rust on flags, like padlock keys
Down rock bottom. Woman sits her frame,
Body lies below. The gong again gongs.
Children’s children dance in blindfold play.

Inspired by the Novodevichy Cemetery monuments, the trees of love, and Mihail Chemiakin’s sculpture group ‘Children are the Victims of Adult Vices’

To Melancholy and His Train

He is my art, my muse and genius, surging
With passion dark, my heart’s black humour, flushing
Black wash, black ink, sublime inscription, rushing
From woe, its source in sweat from face that’s gaunt.

Who makes this self? The suspect suitor, forging
Meaning, true paradox, most witty, meaning
My debt to him, the inspiration, preening
His flustered feather quills, his plume at taunt.

I shall not shake my misery by hand,
And heart to heart imbibe its influence,
Nor bear with pride its suffocating band,

Most tantalizing loose, but sluggish, dense,
And smotheringly weighty as wet sand.
No: I am owed not this, but recompense.

Inspired by the desperate rationalizations of bad motivational speakers.

The Convergence of the Twain

That grace was some incongruous cartoon
Where lumbar flame and melt and mould, as if
Droop bimetallic broke not sweat nor stiff,
Strong limber, and warped not its cold cocoon.

With model rend, not jigsaw nor balloon,
And graded dive, and blend and bled flame swift,
And seemed to slide and, liquid, through sieve sift,
It leered and led through lead its nameless wound.

After Hardy and Armitage
