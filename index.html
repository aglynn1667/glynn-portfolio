Advanced Practice Manual for Bone & Joint Infections

Division of Infectious Diseases
University of Louisville

This manual was created by the Division of Infectious Diseases at the University of Louisville to establish the standard of practice for the care of patients with bone and joint infections, skin and soft tissue infections, and bacteremia.
