XVI. In Paradisum

In paradisum deducant angeli.
In tuo adventu suscipiant te martyres,
Et perducant te in civitatem sanctam Ierusalem,
Chorus angelorum te suscipiat,
Et cum Lazaro quondam paupere
Aeternam habeas requiem.

 

Let the angels lead you into paradise. Let the martyrs receive you at your coming and lead you into the holy city of Jerusalem. Let the choir of angels receive you, and with the once poor Lazarus, may you have eternal rest.
