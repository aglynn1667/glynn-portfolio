X. Domine Iesu Christe

X. Domine Iesu Christe

Domine Iesu Christe, rex gloriae,
Libera animas omnium fidelium defunctorum
De poenis inferni, et profundo lacu:
Libera eas de ore leonis,
Ne absorbeat eas tartarus,
Ne cadant in obscurum,
Sed signifer sanctus Michael
Repraesentet eas in lucem sanctam:
Quam olim Abrahae promisisti,
Et semini eius.

 

Lord Jesus Christ, King of Glory, free the souls of all the faithful departed from infernal punishment and the deep pit. Free them from the mouth of the lion. Do not let hell swallow them; do not let them fall into darkness; but let the holy standard-bearer Michael lead them into holy light, as once you promised to Abraham and his seed.
