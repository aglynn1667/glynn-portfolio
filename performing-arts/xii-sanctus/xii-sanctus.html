XII. Sanctus

Sanctus, Sanctus, Sanctus,
Dominus Deus Sabaoth.
Pleni sunt coeli et terra Gloria tua,
Osanna in excelsis.

 

Holy, holy, holy, Lord God of Hosts. Heaven and earth are full of your glory, Savior in the highest.
