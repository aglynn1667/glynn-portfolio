Requiem

VIII. Lacrimosa

VIII. Lacrimosa: the Text

Lacrimosa dies illa,
Qua resurget ex favilla
Iudicandus homo reus.
Huic ero parce, Deus.

Pie Iesu Domine,
Dona eis Requiem.

Tearful will be that day, on which from the ashes will rise the guilty man who is to be judged. Therefore spare him, O God.

Merciful Lord Jesus, grant them rest.
