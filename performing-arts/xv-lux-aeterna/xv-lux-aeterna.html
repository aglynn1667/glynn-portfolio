XV. Lux Aeterna

Lux aeterna luceat eis, Domine:
Cum sanctis tuis in aeternum,
Quia pius es.

Requiem aeternam dona eis, Domine,
Et lux perpetua luceat eis,
Quia pius es.

 

Let eternal light shine upon them, Lord, with your saints forever, for you are merciful.

Grant them eternal rest, Lord, and let perpetual light shine upon them, for you are merciful.
