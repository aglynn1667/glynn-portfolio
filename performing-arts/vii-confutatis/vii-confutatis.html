VII. Confutatis

VII. Confutatis

Confutatis maledictis,
Flammis acribus addictis,
Voca me cum benedictis.

Oro supplex et acclinis,
Cor Contritum quasi cinis:
Gere curam mei finis.

 

Once the cursed have been convicted, consigned to bitter flames, name me among the blessed.

Kneeling and bowed, I pray; my heart is crushed like ashes. Take care of my end.
