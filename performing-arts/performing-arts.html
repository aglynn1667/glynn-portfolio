Musical Work

In parallel to the work detailed in my CV, I have extensive experience in the performing arts, primarily music.

My musical career began at age 5 with my first piano lesson and continues to this day. My most recent public performance was my final recital as an organ scholar at the Rockefeller Memorial Chapel (pictured here).

I have in recent years turned firmly towards choral and organ music, working as an organist, choral scholar, and conductor in various ecclesiastical settings. Likewise, my recent compositions have primarily taken the form of choral sacred music.
