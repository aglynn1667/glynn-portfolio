Fugue on a Theme by Oliver

for String Quartet

The Fall of Gil-galad:

The subject of the fugue is the melody of ‘The Fall of Gil-galad’, sung by Samwise Gamgee in the BBC's 1981 radio adaptation of J.R.R. Tolkien's The Lord of the Rings.

The music for the series was written by Stephen Oliver, and this particular melody was sung by Bill Nighy, who voiced Gamgee.
