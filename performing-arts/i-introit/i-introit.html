Requiem

I. Introit

I. Introit: the Text

Requiem aeternam dona eis domine:
Et lux perpetua luceat eis.
Te decet hymnus, Deus, in Sion,
Et tibi reddetur votum in Jerusalem:
Exaudi orationem meam,
Ad te omnis caro veniet.
Requiem aeternam dona eis domine,
Et lux perpetua luceat eis.

Grant them eternal rest, Lord, and let perpetual light shine upon them. A hymn becomes you, O God, in Zion, and a pledge shall be given to you in Jerusalem: hear my prayer; all flesh shall come to you. Grant them eternal rest, Lord, and let perpetual light shine upon them.
