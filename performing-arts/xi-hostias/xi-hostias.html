XI. Hostias

Hostias et preces tibi, Domine,
Laudis offerimus:
Tu suscipe pro animabus illis,
Quarum hodie memoriam facimus.
Fac eas, Domine, de morte transire ad vitam,
Quam olim Abrahae promisisti,
Et semini eius.

 

Lord, we offer you sacrifices and prayers of praise: accept them on behalf of those souls whom today we commit to memory. Make them, Lord, cross from death to life, as once you promised to Abraham and his seed.
