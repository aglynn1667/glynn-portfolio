V. Rex Tremendae

V. Rex Tremendae: the Text

Rex tremendae maiestatis,
Qui salvandos salvas gratis,
Salva me, fons pietatis.

Recordare Iesu pie,
Quod sum causa tuae viae:
Ne me perdas illa die.

Quaerens me, sedisti lassus:
Redemisti crucem passus:
Tantus labor non sit cassus.

Iuste iudex ultionis,
Donum fac remissionis,
Ante diem rationis.

King of terrible majesty, who out of kindness saves those who are to be saved, save me, fountain of mercy.

Remember, merciful Jesus, that I am the cause of your way, lest you lose me on that day.

Seeking me, you sat weary; having suffered the cross, you redeemed me: do not let such great toil be in vain.

Just judge of vengeance, make a gift of remission before the day of reckoning.
